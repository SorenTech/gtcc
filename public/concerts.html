Dec 7 & 9

Comfort and Joy

December 7, 2018 @ 7:30PM | St. John the Baptist Church, Westport, MA

December 9, 2018 @ 3:00PM | St. Theresa’s Catholic Church, Tiverton, RI

Come and enjoy a collection of Christmas music, both familiar and new! This concert will feature pieces by JS Bach, GF Handel, Ralph Vaughn Williams, Samuel Scheidt, and others. We will sing some familiar carols in unfamiliar settings and showcase a piece by a local composer, Will Ayton (former chair of the music department at Roger Williams University). Our accompanist, Melissa Woolverton, will be joined for this concert by a brass quartet. Keeping our tradition alive, we will invite the audience to join us in singing Handel’s Hallelujah Chorus at the conclusion of the concert. We hope you’ll be there.

Tickets: $12 in advance, $15 at the door. Tickets available from Chorus Members or may be purchased online, at Partner’s Village Store in Westport, or at Coastal Roasters Coffee in Tiverton.
